Now, Let's assume following is our floor plan with four dwelling units per floor


Once we have our floor plan in place, following guidelines are to be followed for calculating FSI area.



FSI area (Green Color) per floor for our building is 295 Sq.Mt.

Let's assume our buildings are 14 storied high.

Therefore, total FSI area of one building will be 295 * 14 = 4130 Sq.Mt.

So, if we decided to utilize all the permissible FSI, the number of buildings we can have = 27000 / 4130 = 6.5
